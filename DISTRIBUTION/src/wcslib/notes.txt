Steps to obtain the necessary source files from the WCSLIB distribution.
(valid for WCSLIB 4.5)
==========================================================================

- download the current WCSLIB distribution and unpack it in a
  separate directory.

- there:

  ./configure --without-pgplot --without-cfitsio --disable-fortran
              --disable-utils --disable-largefile

- gmake

(the previous steps are necessary to also obtain the .c versions of the .l
 sources and to generate wcsconfig.h)

- copy the following files from ?/wcslib-???/C/ to this directory:
    cel.c cel.h lin.c lin.h
    log.c log.h prj.c prj.h spc.c spc.h sph.c sph.h spx.c spx.h tab.c tab.h
    wcs.c wcsfix.c wcsfix.h wcs.h wcshdr.c wcshdr.h wcsmath.h
    wcstrig.c wcstrig.h wcsulex.c wcsunits.c wcsunits.h wcsutil.c wcsutil.h
    wcsutrn.c

- copy wcsconfig.h from ?/wcslib-???/ to this directory.

- edit wcsconfig.h and _remove_ the line with 'HAVE_SINCOS'
  (setting the symbol to zero is not sufficient)
  This is to prevent installation problems on machines that do not
  have the GNU extension sincos.
